# Terms

## Core Terms

- `source of truth`: the one canonical place where a contract or state is defined
- `overlay`: a project-local specialization layered on top of reusable governance
- `vertical slice`: a boundary that can be understood from entry point to final effect
- `rollback posture`: the documented way to contain or reverse a failed change
- `flow-first`: structure where folders communicate business or system flow
- `contract`: the explicit boundary another human or machine can rely on
